What Are Block Themes?

Block themes represent a fundamental shift in WordPress development. Introduced with Full Site Editing (FSE) in WordPress 5.9 and refined through version 6.0 and beyond, block themes allow you to design and customize every part of your website—from headers to footers, from blog archives to single posts—using the intuitive block editor interface.

Unlike traditional themes that rely on PHP template files and require coding knowledge to customize, block themes use a visual editor for all aspects of your site. This means you can modify your site's layout, typography, colors, and structure without ever touching a line of code.

The Power of Full Site Editing

Full Site Editing is more than just a feature—it's a complete paradigm shift. Here's what makes it revolutionary:

Visual Control Over Everything

With FSE, the familiar block editor extends beyond just post and page content. You can now edit your site's header, footer, sidebar, and other template parts visually. Want to move your navigation menu from the top to the side? Simply drag and drop. Need to adjust your footer layout? Click and customize.

Global Styles System

The global styles interface gives you unprecedented control over your site's design system. Change your entire site's color palette with a few clicks. Adjust typography across all headings at once. Create and save multiple style variations for different sections of your site.

Template Flexibility

Block themes come with a collection of templates and template parts that you can mix, match, and modify to suit your needs. Whether you're building a blog, a business site, or an online portfolio, you have complete freedom to structure your pages exactly how you envision them.

Why Block Themes Are Perfect for Modern WordPress

No Code Required

The most significant advantage? You don't need to be a developer. Block themes democratize web design, making it accessible to content creators, small business owners, and anyone with a vision for their website. The visual interface means what you see is what you get.

Performance by Design

Block themes are built on modern web standards and are optimized for performance. Without the bloat of traditional page builders or complex PHP processing, sites built with block themes typically achieve exceptional Core Web Vitals scores. This means faster load times, better SEO, and happier visitors.

Future-Proof Architecture

As WordPress continues to evolve, block themes are at the center of that evolution. By building with blocks, you're investing in a platform that's actively developed and improved with each WordPress release. Your site will benefit from new features and enhancements automatically.

Getting Started with Block Themes

Ready to experience the future of WordPress? Here's how to begin:

  1. Ensure You're Running WordPress 6.0 or Later: Block themes require Full Site Editing capabilities.
  2. Choose a Quality Block Theme: Look for themes with extensive pattern libraries, good documentation, and active development. Moiraine checks all these boxes.
  3. Explore the Site Editor: Navigate to Appearance → Editor in your WordPress dashboard. This is your new design playground.
  4. Start with Patterns: Browse the pattern library and insert pre-designed sections to quickly build out your pages.
  5. Customize Global Styles: Use the Styles panel to adjust colors, typography, and spacing to match your brand.
  6. Experiment Fearlessly: The beauty of block themes is that changes are non-destructive. Try different layouts, test various color schemes, and find what works best.
