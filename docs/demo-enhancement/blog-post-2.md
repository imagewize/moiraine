## What Are Block Patterns?

Block patterns are pre-designed combinations of WordPress blocks arranged in aesthetically pleasing, functional layouts. Think of them as design templates for specific sections of your website. Instead of starting with a blank canvas every time you need a hero section, testimonial showcase, or pricing table, you simply insert a pattern and customize it with your content.

The magic of patterns lies in their flexibility. While they provide professional design out of the box, every element is fully customizable through WordPress's block editor. Change colors, adjust spacing, swap images, modify text—all without touching a line of code.
